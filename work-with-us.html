Work With Us

We partner with brands, organisations and individuals who share our commitment to genuinely beneficial, ethically produced digital wellness content. If that sounds like you we would love to talk.

Who We Reach

Our Audience

The Etherical Media network reaches a growing audience of wellness-conscious, thoughtful individuals across the UK and internationally — people who are actively seeking better tools, resources and information to improve their mental, emotional and physical wellbeing.

Our audience is characterised by genuine engagement and genuine intent. They arrive at our properties because they have a specific need — a difficult decision to navigate, a sleep challenge to address, a day they want to approach with greater intention — and they leave having genuinely used what we built. This makes them an unusually receptive audience for brands that serve their actual needs.

How We Work Together

📣

What We Require

Our Partnership Values

We are selective about the partnerships we pursue. Not because we are difficult to work with — but because our audience trusts us, and we take that trust seriously. Every partner we work with must meet the following criteria:

✦

Genuine Benefit to Our Audience

The product, service or content must genuinely serve the needs and interests of our audience — not just be tangentially related to wellness.

✦

Honest, Substantiated Claims

All claims made in partnership content must be honest, evidence-based and clearly within what the product or service can genuinely deliver.

✦

Transparent Disclosure

All commercial relationships are clearly disclosed to our audience. We never present paid content as independent editorial without explicit labelling.

✦

Ethical Business Practices

We will not partner with organisations whose business practices conflict with our values of honesty, privacy and genuine human benefit.

✦

Respect for Our Editorial Independence

Commercial partners do not direct editorial content. Our independent voice is what makes our audience trust us — and that is what makes partnership with us valuable.
