Our Portfolio Philosophy

How We Choose What to Build

Every Etherical Media property begins with a simple but demanding question: does the world genuinely need this? Not does a market exist for it, not can we rank for it, not will it generate traffic — but will it make a real, meaningful difference to the people who use it?

That question filters out the vast majority of ideas. What remains is a portfolio of tools and resources that we are genuinely proud of — each one addressing a real human need with genuine research, exceptional design and honest purpose.

01

Real Human Need

The property must address a genuine, well-evidenced human need — not a manufactured one.

02

Research Grounded

Content and tools must be informed by credible research in relevant fields — psychology, neuroscience, behavioural science.

03

Design Excellence

The experience must be beautiful, intuitive and genuinely pleasurable to use — not just functional.

04

Responsible Scope

We only add properties we can maintain to the same standard as the rest of the portfolio.

MomentMantra

momentmantra.com

"Three words. One breath. A mantra made for you."

Visit Site →

What It Does

Moment Mantra is a free AI-powered daily mantra generator. Users enter three honest words describing how they feel in this moment and receive a unique, personalised mantra crafted specifically for them — acknowledging their actual emotional state and offering warm, compassionate encouragement.

What Makes It Different

Unlike generic affirmation apps that serve the same phrases to everyone, every Moment Mantra is created fresh and is completely unique. The approach is rooted in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy and Compassion-Focused psychology — meeting people where they genuinely are rather than simply overwriting difficult feelings with positivity.

Key Features

  • AI-generated personalised mantras in real time
  • Rooted in neuroscience and ACT psychology
  • Free to use — no account required
  • In-depth articles on mantra science and mindfulness

Flipin'Choice

flipinchoice.com

"The coin doesn't decide. It reveals."

Visit Site →

What It Does

Flipin' Choice is a coin flip decision-making tool grounded in the psychology of gut reaction. Users enter their two options, flip the coin and then pay attention to how they feel when it lands — relieved or disappointed. That emotional response reveals the decision they had already made subconsciously.

What Makes It Different

Flipin' Choice is not about letting chance decide — it is about using the structure of a coin flip to access the subconscious preferences that analytical thinking so often obscures. The name works on two levels — British slang for exasperation and literally what you do — capturing the universal experience of being genuinely stuck.

Key Features

  • Dramatic animated coin flip experience
  • Gut reaction verdict system — relieved or disappointed
  • In-depth articles on decision psychology and intuition
  • Free to use — no account required

What It Does

Somnuscape is our most comprehensive property — a complete sleep and dream wellness platform combining a personalised sleep ritual generator, a dream pattern analyser, free and premium printable resources and an extensive library of evidence-based articles on sleep science, dream psychology and lucid dreaming.

The platform features a beautiful light and dark mode, animated canvas design and tools that have been praised for their warmth and depth of insight.

Key Features
